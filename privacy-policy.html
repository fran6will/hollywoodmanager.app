Gameplay Data

We store data generated through gameplay, including: agency statistics and reputation, actor rosters and training history, casting submissions and audition results, financial transactions (in-game currency), notifications and alerts, trade offers, and league standings.

Purchase Data

In-app purchases (rescue tokens, cash injections, ad removal) are processed through RevenueCat, which receives transaction data from the App Store or Google Play. We receive purchase status and entitlements but do not store payment card details.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide the game experience — authenticate your account, save progress, synchronize multiplayer state
  • Communicate with you — push notifications for in-game events (configurable in Settings)
  • Display ads — serve ads via Google AdMob; personalized only with your ATT consent
  • Process purchases — fulfill in-app purchases via RevenueCat
  • Improve the app — understand gameplay patterns to balance game mechanics

4. Multiplayer Visibility

Hollywood Talent Manager is a multiplayer game. The following data is visible to other players within your league:

  • Agency name and display name
  • Agency logo (if uploaded)
  • League standings, reputation, and win/loss statistics
  • Actor roster composition

Your email address is never visible to other players.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. Gameplay data (notifications, expired offers, resolved events) may be periodically pruned for performance, but core account and progress data persists until you delete your account.

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings > Delete Account within the app. This permanently deletes:

  • Your authentication account
  • Your agency and all associated gameplay data
  • All actors, submissions, transactions, and notifications
  • Any uploaded images (agency logo)

Deletion is immediate and irreversible. Data held by third-party services (RevenueCat purchase history, AdMob logs) is subject to those services' own retention policies.

7. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS. Authentication tokens are stored securely on-device using platform-native secure storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android). Database access is controlled through Row Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring users can only access their own data.

8. Children's Privacy

Hollywood Talent Manager is rated 17+ and is not directed at children under 17. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Tracking & Advertising

The App uses Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You will be asked for permission before any tracking occurs. If you decline, ads will still be shown but will not be personalized based on your activity across other apps and websites.

You can change your tracking preference at any time in your device's Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (see Section 6)
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

11. Changes to This Policy
