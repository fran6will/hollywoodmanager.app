Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes your account, agency, and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

I'm not receiving push notifications

Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings for Hollywood Talent Manager. In the app, check Settings to verify your notification preferences are turned on. If you recently updated via TestFlight, restarting the app will re-register your push token.

How do I restore my purchases?

In-app purchases are tied to your App Store or Google Play account. Go to Settings > Restore Purchases to restore any previous purchases on a new device.

Can I transfer my account to another device?

Yes. Simply sign in with the same email address on your new device. Your agency and all progress will sync automatically.

How does multiplayer work?

Hollywood Talent Manager is an asynchronous multiplayer game. You compete in a shared league alongside other human players and AI agencies. No lobby or scheduling needed — drop in and play anytime.

My actor left my agency. Can I get them back?

When an actor's patience reaches zero, they become available for rescue (if you have rescue tokens) or leave your agency. Released actors may appear in the free agent market where any agency can sign them.
